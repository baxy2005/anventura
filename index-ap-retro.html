Aventura Retro Apartment Budapest

Aventura Retro Apartment Budapest is a fully equipped two bedroom apartment one metro stop from Aventura Boutique Hostel, right in the middle of Budapest downtown, two minutes from the Basilica, Opera House and the Andrassy Avenue. Check-in is in the hostel! It is ideal for families with bigger children or for group of friends (maximum 4 people).

 

Retro Apartment is in fact a real home with the atmosphere of the 60s. Here you can experience a stylish Hungarian home atmosphere among solid Old World wooden furniture accented with traditional Hungarian wallpaper featuring a charming floral pattern.

 

The apartment has a bathroom with bathtub, a separate toilet and a spacious kitchen equipped with fridge, microwave oven, stove and kettle. There are three rooms: a small bedroom with a double bed, an anteroom with two single beds and a living room with a large sofa. The living room has a balcony as well. The flat is equipped with Wi-Fi, cable TV, computer and telephone with free unlimited local calls.

Location of Art Apartment

Retro Apartment is located on the Pest side of the city, in the middle of downtown on Metro line 3 (blue line) at “Arany János” Station, 1 metro stop from Aventura Boutique Hostel. Several sites are within walking distance.

 

The St Stephen Basilica, the Opera House and the Andrassy Avenue is just around the corner; the Parliament is just few minute walk away. The busy Nagymező street and Kazinczy street (full of bars, clubs and cafés) is also just few corners away. Post office, exchange offices, banks and grocery stores are all few minutes away.

 

There are many kind of different eateries/restaurants from cheap to first class places in the area.

Address of Retro Apartment: District 6, 3 Bajcsy Zsilinszky köz., 3rd floor/apt. 1 Check-in is at Aventura Boutique Hostel (12 Visegradi str.). See directions to the hostel. Retro Apartment is one metro stop from the hostel.
