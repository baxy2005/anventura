Aventura Boutique Hostel Budapest is very well located on the Pest side of the city, 2 minutes from the Nyugati station of the blue line metro, and close to the Margaret Island.

The following sites are all within 10-15 minute walk

  • Basilica
  • Oktogon and Andrassy Avenue
  • Opera House
  • House of Terror

How to get to the hostel

Aventura Boutique Hostel Budapest

From Budapest "Nyugati" Metro Station (Blue line – Line 2)

From the metro level take the escalator towards the „Trains”/„Váci Út”. Then exit towards Katona József utca (the steps on the left) and keep walking on Katona József utca for 200 meters until you reach Visegrádi utca 12. Our building is the big white one on the left opposite corner. Entrance is next to the coffee shop. We have a light box at the gate with our logo and a separate video intercom.

Few useful tips

  • Always have a validated ticket on the public transports! Controllers are everywhere and the fine is more than 50 EUR!
  • NEVER TAKE A TAXI ON THE STREET! If you would like to come by taxi, CALL this number: 6x6 Taxi 00-36-1-666-6666. By calling taxi on phone, you can avoid overcharges!

Arriving by Car

Our street is a one way street. You can park your car on the guarded open air parking place behind the hostel (on the corner of Katona József utca and Hegedűs Gyula utca). It costs about HUF 5000 for 24 hours. Parking on the streets cost aprox. 1 EUR/hour between 8 am and 8 pm, and you cannot pay more than 3 hour at a time.
