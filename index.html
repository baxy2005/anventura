About Us

Aventura Boutique Hostel Budapest is a small 4 room family run hostel on the Pest side of Budapest 2 minutes from “Nyugati” metro station and few minutes from Margaret Island. Numerous attractions are within 15-20 min walk, and all kind of facilities are available in the area.

Directions Location

Aventura Boutique Hostel Budapest is located on the Pest side of Budapest 2 minutes from “Nyugati” metro station and few minutes from Margaret Island in a popular, traditional residential neighbourhood. Numerous attractions are within 15-20 min walk, and all kind of facilities are available in the area.

Address
12 Visegradi utca (St.),
1st floor/apt 1
Budapest, District 13

Directions from "Nyugati Tér" metro stationb
Only a short walk to our Aventura Hostel Budapest: Exit towards Szent István körút (boulevard). Start walking towards the river, and our street (Visegrádi) is the first one on the right. Come till no 12. It is a big white corner building on the left side of the street. There is a small lightbox on the house showing our logo (a letter A in a green house).

You can easily reach Aventura Hostel Budapest from any point of city by the following public transport (The name of the closest stop is “Nyugati tér” / Western Train Station):

Metro 3 (blue line)
Tram: 4, 6
Bus: 9, 26, 91, 109, 191, 266, 291
Trolley bus: 72, 73
Night bus: 914-914A, 923, 931, 934, 950-950A
