Aventura Apartment Rouge Budapest

Apartment Rouge is our cheapest and smallest apartment located centrally on the Pest side of the city. It is an ideal romantic hideout for couples (max two people) who love privacy and comfortable lodgings.

 

The apartment has a bathroom with shower and toilet inside, a small but well equipped kitchen with fridge, stove and kettle. There is a large room with a double bed and a sofa bed. The room has a warm, cosy, romantic atmosphere with reddish, maroon colours and a nice antique wardrobe. Flat is equipped with fast Wi-Fi and telephone with unlimited local landline calls.

 

It is located near the Aventura Boutique Hostel (check in here) in the same street, only 2 minutes walk/140 meters away, so you are able to hop over anytime for a breakfast or for a chat with other travellers. You can also come over to use other facilities in the hostel – like computer, laundry service, tour info. The 24 hour hostel reception is always at your service.

Location of Apartment Rouge

Apartment Rouge is located on the Pest side of the city, on Metro line 3 (blue line) at “Nyugati” Station, 2 minutes from Aventura Boutique Hostel in a nice, less touristy neighbourhood.

 

Several sites are within walking distance. The Margaret Island, the Basilica, the Opera House, the Parliament, etc. are all within 10-15 minutes walk away. The busy Nagymező street (full of bars, clubs and cafés) is also just few corners away. Post office, exchange offices, banks and grocery stores are all few minutes away.

 

There are many kind of different eateries/restaurants from cheap to first class places in the area.

Address of Apartment Rouge: District 13, 15 Visegrádi str., between the 2nd and 3rd floor Check-in is at Aventura Boutique Hostel (12 Visegradi str.). See directions to the hostel.
Apartment Rouge is only 2 minutes (140 meters) walk away from the hostel in the same street.
