Africa room

Africa room shows off the adventurous ambience of Africa with giraffe pattern on the wall and a nice dark wooden loft instead of bunk beds. There are 4 beds downstairs on the floor and 4 mattresses on the loft. The lockers are under the beds Ð huge drawers, where you can store your whole backpack, and for the people on the loft there are big military boxes functioning as lockers. (Padlocks can be borrowed from the reception for a 5 EUR deposit.) We have new blinds on the windows, so you can make the room completely dark if you wish to sleep longer in the morning or if you are disturbed by the streetlights.
